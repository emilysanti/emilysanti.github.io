Fees

Learning Disability Assessment - $2800.

Dyslexia (reading disorder), Dysgraphia (writing disorder), Dyscalculia (math disability) testing.

Testing for a learning disability requires 3 days of testing, and completion of medical and behavioral forms. The teacher will be contacted to complete academic and behavioral forms. Fee includes pre and post conferences, all testing, evaluation of school records, and a written report with recommendations.

ADD/ADHD - Attention deficit disorder with or without hyperactivity - $3000.

Requires an observation of your child in the classroom, 3 days of testing, and completion of medical and behavioral forms. The teacher will be contacted to complete academic and behavioral forms. Fee includes pre and post conferences, testing, observation in school, evaluation of school records, speaking with school personnel, and a written report with recommendations.
