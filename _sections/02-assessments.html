Educational Assessments

Learning difficulties can make children feel frustrated and unmotivated. An educational assessment uncovers the reasons why your child is having challenges in school and provides data regarding the intellectual, academic, behavioral, and social-emotional needs of your child. Reports are provided after all assessments, and children have the option to develop a presentation about themselves that they can present to their school team.
