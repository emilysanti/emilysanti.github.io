Training & Consultation

Parent coaching and consultation is available, as well as training sessions for schools and communities.

Training sessions and consultations aim to promote and educate individuals on using strength-based approaches when empowering children and removing barriers to learning. Areas that can be covered include:

  • Dyslexia
  • Fetal alcohol spectrum disorders (FASD)
  • Selective mutism
  • Addressing chronic behavior challenges
  • Mindfulness strategies for families
  • Special education systems and community partnerships
  • Differentiating instruction
