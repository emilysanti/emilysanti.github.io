About Emily

Emily Santiago is a licensed Educational Psychologist, specializing in diagnostic educational assessments and intelligence testing of children from 5 to 17 years. She holds a credential for K-8 teaching, with experience in public and private schools across the USA and abroad.

Licenses and Certifications

Emily is licensed by the California Board of Behavioral Sciences (license number LEP3642) and a Nationally Certified School Psychologist.
