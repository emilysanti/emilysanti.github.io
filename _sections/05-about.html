About Emily

Emily Santiago (LEP, NCSP) is an Educational Psychologist specializing in diagnostic assessments and intelligence testing of kids from 5 to 17 years. She is passionate about providing children and families with strength based recommendations designed to help kids reach their full potential. With 20+ years as an educator and school psychologist, Emily has helped many children overcome challenges and reach successful outcomes.

Licenses and Certifications

Emily is licensed by the California Board of Behavioral Sciences (license number LEP3642) and is a Nationally Certified School Psychologist. She is an active member of the National Association of School Psychologists and the American Psychological Association.

School Psychologist

Emily has practiced as a school psychologist since 2009 and currently teaches in the Educational Psychology department at California State University East Bay. She has experience developing mindfulness programs, school-based mental health services, and family wraparound services.

Blogger

Emily regularly posts on Medium, where she shares her thoughts and strategies for families and individuals.
